Privacy Policy

Effective: March 1, 2026

Vault is a watch collection management app developed by Chad Newbry, LLC. Your privacy matters to us — here's exactly how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Vault stores the data you enter — your watch collection details, wear history, wishlist items, and insurance documents. All of this data is stored locally on your device.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not transmit your data to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Vault does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data. If you delete the app, your data is removed from the device.

Children's Privacy

Vault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
