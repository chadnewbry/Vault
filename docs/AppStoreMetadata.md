## Description

Your watch collection deserves better than a spreadsheet.

Vault is a beautifully designed app for watch enthusiasts who want to catalog, track, and manage every timepiece they own — from daily beaters to grail pieces.

**Build Your Collection**
Add watches with detailed specs, photos, purchase info, and current market value. Whether you own three watches or thirty, Vault keeps everything organized and instantly accessible.

**Track Value Over Time**
See how your collection's worth changes at a glance. Log purchase prices, track current values, and understand your portfolio — useful for insurance, resale, or just satisfying curiosity.

**Log Your Wear History**
Record which watches you wear and when. Spot your favorites, rotate more evenly, or finally prove that your dress watch isn't just gathering dust.

**Store Insurance Documents**
Attach receipts, appraisals, warranty cards, and insurance docs directly to each watch. Everything you need, right where you need it.

**Premium Design, One-Time Purchase**
No subscriptions. No recurring fees. Unlock Vault Premium once for $6.99 and get full access forever — while competitors charge you monthly just to view your own collection.

Built for collectors who care about their watches as much as the details.
